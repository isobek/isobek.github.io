Take an O.F.F. Day at Olivet!

May 6th, 2017 — All Afternoon

Olivet Baptist Church, 2312 Centre Ave

The 2nd annual Olivet Fun Fest (O.F.F. DAY!) will be a one-day musical event in Pittsburgh’s Hill District, featuring local musicians. All proceeds from tickets and refreshments will go to benefit Olivet Baptist, helping the church reconnect with its community and share its rich history with Pittsburgh.
